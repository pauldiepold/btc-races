  Additional Information
  What is the source of the email addresses that you use for sending your messages?
  Recipients are exclusively active members of Berlin Track Club e.V.
  Their email addresses originate from our official membership database
  (Campai), which members provided themselves when joining the club and
  agreed to be contacted at for club-related purposes. We do not purchase,
  rent, scrape or otherwise externally acquire address lists. The user
  table in our application is synchronized from Campai via a server-side
  job, so only currently active members can receive emails.

  How do you currently manage and remove email addresses that have
  unsubscribed or resulted in bounce backs from your mailing list?
  Because we send only transactional, user-initiated messages (login
  links and registration confirmations) to authenticated club members,
  there is no marketing list and no unsubscribe link is required by law
  for this category of mail. Address hygiene is handled on two levels:

  1. Source-of-truth sync: when a member updates their email address or
     leaves the club in our membership system (Campai), the change is
     propagated to the application database via the member-sync job, so
     outdated or revoked addresses stop receiving mail automatically.

  2. Automated bounce handling: hard bounces and persistent delivery
     failures reported by Azure Communication Services are processed
     automatically and the affected address is suppressed from further
     sends, in addition to the manual hygiene performed via Campai when
     a member reports a delivery problem directly.
